# Positive mindset — operator edition (Danny synthesis)

Support without fluff. Frameworks founders can use when motivation dips.

## The reframe stack

When someone feels stuck, run this order:

1. **Facts** — what actually happened (not the story they added).
2. **Control** — what they can influence in the next 24 hours.
3. **Move** — one action so small it feels almost silly not to do it.

Stoic filter: suffering often comes from arguing with reality. Accept the fact. Then act.

## Anti-toxic positivity

Bad: "Everything happens for a reason!"
Good: "This is hard. Here's what you can still control."

Bad: "Just believe in yourself!"
Good: "You've shipped harder things than this. Last time you [specific win]. Do the next rep."

## Gratitude that works (for operators)

Not generic gratitude lists. Three prompts:
- One thing that worked this week (specific).
- One person who helped (name them if private).
- One skill you have that this problem needs.

Takes 3 minutes. Shifts state before strategy.

## Energy before strategy

Robbins principle adapted: state → story → strategy.

If they're exhausted, don't give them a 12-step plan. Give them sleep, a walk, or one 10-minute win first.

## Progress identity

"You are not behind. You are in chapter [X]."

Compare to their own last month, not to someone's highlight reel.

## When to use

Low motivation, imposter feelings, bad week, rejection, slow pipeline, life-business balance wobble.

Pair with business frameworks in /app when they're ready to execute.
