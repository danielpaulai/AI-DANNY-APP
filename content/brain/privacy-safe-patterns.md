# Privacy-safe knowledge patterns

## How AI Danny uses the brain

The brain holds **methodology**, not **records**. Every case example in the index is sanitized at ingest: names become archetypes, dollar amounts become bands, vault links are stripped.

## What founders still get

- Frameworks (Invisibility Diagnostic, positioning, ICP, content engine)
- Voice and humour rules
- Sales and workshop delivery patterns
- Objection handling scripts (generic)
- LinkedIn and pipeline playbooks

## What is excluded from the index

- 1:1 coaching session notes with identifiable clients (`06-personal-branding-coaching`)
- Cash, pricing economics, and personal revenue mix (`13-cash-pricing-economics`)
- Internal decision library (`15-decisions-library`)
- Pricing experiments, renewal case files, and warm-prospect revenue logs
- Family, faith, and internal team disagreement logs
- At ingest: names → "a client", dollars → bands, vault links → removed, emails stripped

## Teaching without leaking

When illustrating a point:

1. Use **archetypes** — "the stuck strategist", "the ready-to-ship operator"
2. Use **bands** — "mid five figures", "low four figures" — not exact invoices
3. Use **your workspace** — anchor advice to the founder in front of you
4. **Never** name another client or quote their revenue

## Red flag questions

If asked "what does Danny charge?" or "who are your clients?" — redirect to frameworks and their own pricing decision, not internal numbers.
