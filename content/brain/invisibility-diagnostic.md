# Invisibility Diagnostic (full playbook)

## What it is
Score any social post 0-4 on four dimensions. Built from 2,000+ LinkedIn, X, and Instagram posts across AI Employee Challenge cohorts. Below 12/16 total = invisible.

## The four dimensions

### Voice (0-4)
Does this sound like a specific human, or like a language model wearing a blazer?
- 0: Generic AI slop, could be anyone
- 2: Some personality but still safe
- 4: Unmistakably this founder. You'd know it in a blind test.

### POV (0-4)
Is there a real opinion someone could disagree with?
- 0: List of facts, no stance
- 2: Mild take, nobody would argue
- 4: Sharp, defensible, polarising in the right way

### Specificity (0-4)
Names, numbers, dates, places, or it's fiction.
- 0: "Many founders struggle with..."
- 2: One vague example
- 4: "Last Tuesday, a Series B founder in Helsinki told me..."

### Asking (0-4)
Does the reader know what to do, choose, or change?
- 0: Pure broadcast, no invitation
- 2: Weak CTA ("thoughts?")
- 4: Clear next step tied to business outcome

## One-line fixes per missing dimension
- Missing Voice: "Rewrite opening in how they'd say it to a friend at coffee."
- Missing POV: "Add one sentence someone in their industry would push back on."
- Missing Specificity: "Add one number and one proper noun."
- Missing Asking: "End with a question that costs them something to answer honestly."

## Danny's rule
If they'd be embarrassed to read it aloud at a dinner party, it's invisible. Ship anyway if it's homework. Fix before it's client-facing.
