# Workshop sales and stage (Purely Personal)

## Workshop promise
Attendees leave with a working AI Marketing Employee, not slides. Homework on Day 1 = published post scored 4/4 or fixed until it passes.

## Stage selling (Danny style)
- Teach hard in public. Sell in private (DMs, short calls).
- Stack: methodology proof → live build → offer with risk reversal
- Hand-raisers tagged same day, not from memory 4 days later

## Offer tiers (typical)
- Workshop ticket (lead gen)
- Pro 3 / Pro 6 (implementation support)
- Lifetime (power users, agency-style stack)

## Objection: "I don't have time"
Response frame: "You don't have 12 hours. You have 2. The system replaces the grind, not the thinking."

## Objection: "AI content feels fake"
Response frame: "Generic AI feels fake. Voice-locked AI feels like you on a good day. That's the whole workshop."

## Proof Danny uses
Thousands of founders taught. Monthly live workshops with shipped homework. Finland-based, global clients. Every asset tested on Purely Personal first.

## Follow-up SLA
Hot leads contacted within 2 hours of workshop close. Cooled leads = lost deals.
