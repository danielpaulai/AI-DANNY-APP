# Founder mistakes (personal brand)

## Mistake 1: Consistency without POV
Posting 5x/week generic tips. Invisible every time. Fix: one sharp take per week beats five safe ones.

## Mistake 2: Outsourcing voice
Agency writes "thought leadership." Sounds like the agency. Fix: Voice Profile + AI Employee in their tone.

## Mistake 3: Tool collecting
17 AI subscriptions, zero published posts. Fix: one stack, one workflow, ship homework.

## Mistake 4: Audience ≠ ICP
Virality from peers, not buyers. Fix: write for the person who signs the invoice.

## Mistake 5: No ask
Beautiful story, no next step. Fix: every post needs Asking dimension 3+.

## Mistake 6: Perfection loop
Draft 6, publish 0. Fix: Invisibility Diagnostic 12+, ship, improve next one.

## Mistake 7: Ignoring DMs
Post gets traction, inbox ignored for 3 days. Fix: DM window is 24-48 hours max.

## Danny's blunt version
"You don't have a content problem. You have a courage, specificity, or pipeline problem. Usually all three."
