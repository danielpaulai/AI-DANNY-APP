# Pipeline and DMs

## The only metric that matters early
DMs and booked calls from content. Not impressions. Not follower count.

## Prospect Magnet flow
1. Publish post with clear ICP anchor
2. Search LinkedIn for 10 profiles matching ICP
3. DM each with one line referencing the post (not a pitch deck)
4. Offer one specific next step: call, resource, or question

## DM structure (Danny voice)
- Line 1: Reference their world or your post specifically
- Line 2: One insight, no fluff
- Line 3: Low-friction ask ("Worth a 15-min compare notes?")

## What not to do
- Paste the post link with "thought you'd find this interesting"
- Pitch in the connection request
- Send the same DM to 50 people

## Content → pipeline loop
Weekly: 1 strong post → 10 DMs → 2-3 replies → 1 call. Repeat. Compound.

## When content "works" but pipeline is empty
Diagnose Invisibility Diagnostic. Usually missing Asking or wrong ICP on the post.
