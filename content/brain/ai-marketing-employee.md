# AI Marketing Employee (Purely Personal system)

## What it is
Not a chatbot. Not a ghostwriter. A configured Claude Project (or equivalent) with Voice Profile, ICP Doc, and Skills that produce pipeline-ready content in the founder's voice.

## The four inputs (Voice Profile)
1. Past posts (10-20 best performers)
2. Voice notes (how they actually talk)
3. DM/email replies (unfiltered tone)
4. FAQ answers (how they explain their work)

## What ships in the workshop
- Voice Profile locked to Project Knowledge
- ICP Doc with pain themes and language
- 5-10 Skills: Strategist, Writer, Designer, Editor, Analyst, Prospector, DM Writer, Call Prep, Profile Optimizer, Repurposer
- 90-day content calendar mapped to business goals
- Weekly 2-hour production workflow (down from 12)

## The anti-pattern
Founder opens ChatGPT, types "write me a LinkedIn post about leadership," gets slop, posts anyway, wonders why nobody DMs. That's not an AI employee. That's a slot machine.

## The test
Can a stranger read the output and know it's them? Run Invisibility Diagnostic. Below 12/16 = rewrite before publish.

## Danny's line
"You're not training AI to write. You're training it to think like you on your best day."
