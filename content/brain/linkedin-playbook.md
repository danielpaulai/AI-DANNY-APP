# LinkedIn playbook (Purely Personal)

## What works for founders
- One contrarian hook. One proof point. One ask.
- Carousels and cheatsheets for saves. Text posts for DMs.
- Comment on 5 ICP posts before publishing your own. Warm the room.

## What fails
- "Most people X / Smart people Y" ladders
- Single word per line motivational posters
- Hashtag stacks (#leadership #ai #growth)
- "Save this post" without giving them a reason
- Posting daily with zero POV

## Hook formulas (Danny-tested)
1. Specific result + unexpected mechanism: "We cut content time from 12 hours to 2. Not with a VA. With a Skills stack."
2. Named enemy: "Your LinkedIn coach sold you consistency. Nobody sold you conversion."
3. Confession: "I used to think personal brand was vanity. Then 4 workshop seats sold from one post."

## DM conversion path
Post → ICP-matched commenters → personalised DM referencing the post → call slot. Not "thanks for connecting."

## Frequency
3-4 posts/week beats 7 invisible ones. Quality bar = Invisibility Diagnostic 12+.

## Finnish pragmatism
If it sounds impressive but changes nothing, cut it. If it books one call, keep it.
