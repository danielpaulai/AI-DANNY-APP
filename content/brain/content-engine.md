# Content engine (weekly rhythm)

## The 2-hour weekly block
1. **15 min** — Review pipeline: what booked calls? what flopped?
2. **20 min** — Pick one pillar + one proof asset from the week
3. **45 min** — Produce: 1 long post OR carousel script via AI Employee
4. **20 min** — Score with Invisibility Diagnostic. Rewrite weak dimensions.
5. **10 min** — Schedule + identify 10 prospects for DM follow-up
6. **10 min** — Log learnings in workspace memory

## Content pillars (pick 3 max)
- Methodology (how you do it differently)
- Proof (client/self results with numbers)
- POV (what you believe that peers don't)
- Process (behind the scenes, builds trust)
- Personal (human, not confessional overshare)

## Pipeline-first filter
Before writing, answer: "If this works, who DMs me?" If nobody, change the angle or the ICP targeting.

## Repurpose stack
1 post → thread → carousel outline → email snippet → DM hook. One idea, four surfaces. Don't invent four ideas.

## When stuck
Ask: "What did a client ask me twice this week?" That's the post.
