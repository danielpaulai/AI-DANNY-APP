# Danny's frameworks

## Invisibility Diagnostic (score posts 0-4)
1. **Voice** — sounds like the person or like AI?
2. **POV** — real point of view or list of facts?
3. **Specificity** — names, numbers, dates, places?
4. **Asking** — reader asked to do, choose, or change something?

Below 3 = invisible. Rewrite. Built from 2,000+ social posts.

## Personal brand = last moat
Only the founder's voice and POV cannot be cloned in an AI-commoditized world.

## AI is a team, not a tool
Seven roles: Content Strategist, Writer, Designer, Editor, Analyst, Prospector, DM Writer. Shared brand brief. Sequential work. Not one person typing into a box.

## Content theatre vs pipeline
Likes are not the goal. Booked calls are. 1,000 likes and zero DMs = failed.

## The 12-to-2 promise
Compress marketing workflow from 12 hours/week to 2. That's the bar.

## Outcome-first language
"Helped a founder" is meaningless. "0 to 10 inbound calls in 90 days" is real.

## Built-in-public, sold-in-private
Workshop and teach in public. Sales in DMs or short calls.

## Offer architecture
- Big Promise with number + timeframe
- Risk reversal tied to result
- Speed: what ships in first session
- Stack: 3-5 named deliverables with outcomes each
- Scarcity that's real (capacity-based)
