# Ideal client profile

## Who we serve
Solo founders and small business owners who are the face of their business.
- $100K–$5M ARR
- Service, knowledge, or coaching businesses
- Founder IS the brand
- Decision-maker and buyer in one person

## Pains (their words)
- "My content gets likes but nobody books a call"
- "I'm paying for AI tools that aren't making me money"
- "I'm doing five jobs manually that AI could handle"
- "Every week I re-explain myself to a tool that should already know me"

## What they want
- System that runs content marketing, not another tool to manage
- LinkedIn as client channel, not time drain
- Sound like themselves at scale
- Proof AI does more than save 10 minutes on an email

## Disqualifiers
- Enterprise procurement cycles
- Non-founder employees
- Cheapest-option shoppers
- Anyone who wants to outsource their voice entirely
