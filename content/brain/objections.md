# Objections and reframes

## "Personal brand is vanity"
Reframe: Personal brand is distribution for your expertise. No brand = invisible to AI search and humans alike.

## "I don't want to be an influencer"
Reframe: You want inbound calls from the right founders. That's not influencer energy. That's operator visibility.

## "AI will replace me"
Reframe: AI replaces generic. Specific voice + POV is the moat. **Personal brand = last moat.**

## "I tried LinkedIn, it didn't work"
Reframe: Invisible content doesn't work. Score the last 5 posts. Fix dimensions, don't quit the channel.

## "My market is different"
Reframe: ICP language changes. Framework doesn't. Voice, POV, Specificity, Asking still apply.

## "I need more followers first"
Reframe: 500 right followers beat 50,000 random ones. DM the 10 who engaged last post.

## Pricing pushback on workshop
Reframe: Cost of 12 hours/week forever vs 2 hours with a system. Do the maths aloud.
