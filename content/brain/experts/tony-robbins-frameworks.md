# Tony Robbins — state, psychology, and performance (Danny synthesis)

Robbins-level psychology applied to founders stuck in their business — not seminar hype, operational moves.

## State before strategy

Founders make bad offers and worse content when they're in **survival state** (scrambling, comparing, reactive).

**Move before strategy sessions:** 2-minute state break — movement, cold water, walk, box breathing. Sounds soft. Changes the quality of every decision after.

If they're "too busy" for state, they're in a loop. Name it.

## Six human needs (marketing lens)

People buy to meet needs: certainty, variety, significance, connection, growth, contribution.

**Content that converts** hits 2+ needs:
- Significance ("founders like you are invisible because…")
- Growth ("here's the system")
- Connection ("I've been in that 11pm scroll")

**Diagnostic:** Their content is all growth tips (commodity) with no significance or connection (voice).

## RPM — Result focused, Purpose driven, Massive action plan

1. **Result** — what specifically in 90 days?
2. **Purpose** — why must it happen? (Emotional, not "grow the business.")
3. **MAP** — 3–5 weekly actions max, not 40 tasks.

Use in coaching when they're overwhelmed. Cut the MAP until it fits 10 hours/week.

## CANI — constant improvement

1% better in **one lever** per week: hook quality, DM follow-up, offer clarity, show-up rate.

Don't CANI twelve things. Pick the constraint.

## Decision vs preference

"Should I post on LinkedIn?" is a preference loop.

"Will I commit to 3 posts/week for 90 days or kill the channel?" is a decision.

Force decisions at end of every AI Danny session.

## When to cite Robbins

Stuck energy, overwhelm, identity blocks, workshop state management, why they won't execute. Not for funnel math — use Brunson/Hormozi.
