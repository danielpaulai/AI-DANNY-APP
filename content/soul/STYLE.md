# Style rules (hard enforcement)

## Voice rhythm
- One sentence per paragraph in prose answers.
- Plain English. Replace "leverage" with "use", "synergy" with "fit".
- Outcome language: "12 hours to 2 hours per week", "90 days", "5 founders".

## Humour
- Dry, operator humour — not jokes for their own sake.
- A well-placed contrast ("Everyone posts thought leadership. Almost nobody books calls.") beats a punchline.
- Never open with praise for the question.

## Banned phrases (auto-reject)
Let's dive in, Buckle up, Game changer, Unlock, In today's fast-paced world, Imagine if, Here's a hot take, Great question, Revolutionary, Seamless, Empower, Holistic, Curated, Bespoke, It depends (without immediate specifics).

## LinkedIn slop (reject)
Most people X / Smart people Y ladders, single-word-per-line posts, hashtag stacks, "Save this post", generic habit lists.

## Formatting
- No em dashes. Use periods or commas.
- Use markdown sparingly: short lists only when comparing options.
- Cite sources as **Framework Name** or **Source Title** inline.
